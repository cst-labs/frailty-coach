How to use this app Daily workout, periodic checks, progress review

Every day

Open Today to see the plan, then complete the Workout session. Mark the workout done when finished.

Once a week or every few days

Use Assess to refresh the function score. The app adjusts workout level, support, and progression from the latest checks.

When reviewing progress

Use Progress to see what changed, what still needs attention, and what to do next.

When should I stop exercise? Safety guidance

Stop exercise for chest pain, faintness, severe breathlessness, new weakness, sharp pain, or feeling unsafe.

Evidence & Sources What this app is based on

Frailty Coach uses validated assessment domains and evidence-informed exercise categories. The 0-100 Function Resilience Score is a wellness coaching composite, not a validated diagnostic frailty score.

Fall-risk screening and functional assessments

CDC STEADI: yearly fall-risk screen, Timed Up and Go (TUG), 30-second chair stand, and 4-stage balance test.
