Evidence & Sources What this app is based on

Frailty Coach uses validated assessment domains and evidence-informed exercise categories. The 0-100 Function Resilience Score is a wellness coaching composite, not a validated diagnostic frailty score.

Fall-risk screening and functional assessments

CDC STEADI: yearly fall-risk screen, Timed Up and Go, 30-second chair stand, and 4-stage balance test.

CDC STEADI clinical resources

Exercise levels and prescription

Vivifrail functional levels and multicomponent exercise framing; USPSTF guidance on exercise interventions for older adults at increased fall risk.

Vivifrail resources USPSTF falls prevention recommendation

Frailty framing

Clinical Frailty Scale, frailty phenotype, and frailty index informed the app language, but the app does not reproduce or claim to replace those clinical tools.
